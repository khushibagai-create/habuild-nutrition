Family Ambali

For the whole family — together.
SB's take
0:28 · Why this matters
Day 6 — the Saturday family pour. Make one base, let each family member pick their own add-on. Perfect for joint families — everyone's own twist.
Ingredients
Makes
  • 1 cup ragi flour (4x base) ~120 g
  • 4 cup water (for paste) ~480 g
  • 8 glass water (to cook) ~1920 g
  • 4 cup chaas ~480 g
  • Namak to taste
  • Set out individual add-on bowls: chopped coriander, finely chopped green chilli, finely chopped onion, roasted jeera powder, lemon wedges
Method
  1. Make a large smooth paste — ragi plus water
  2. Add 4 glasses water, cook 8-10 min on low flame, stirring throughout
  3. Let it cool
  4. Stir chaas and namak into the base
  5. Pour into individual glasses and let everyone pick their own add-ons
Benefits
Joint-family friendly
Same base, different tastes
Cook once, pour for all
A conversation starter

Ragi Oats Sugar Free Cookies

Mum's go-to evening snack. The trick is roasting the ragi flour slow so the cookies smell nutty, not raw. Dates do all the sweetening — zero sugar.

Ingredients
  • 1 cup ragi (finger millet) flour
  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • 4–5 medjool dates (deseeded, made into a paste)
  • 3 tbsp ghee (or melted coconut oil)
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • ¼ cup mixed nuts (cashew + almond), chopped
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • ½ tsp cardamom powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 2–3 tbsp warm milk (only if dough feels dry)
How to make
  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F)
  2. Dry-roast ragi flour on slow flame 6–7 min until the raw smell goes — set aside
  3. Lightly toast the oats in the same pan 2–3 min
  4. In a bowl, mix ragi flour, oats, sesame seeds, cardamom, salt
  5. Add dates paste, melted ghee and peanut butter — mix till crumbly
  6. Add warm milk 1 tbsp at a time until the dough just comes together (slightly stiff, not wet)
  7. Fold in the chopped nuts
  8. Pinch portions, roll into balls, flatten gently, score an X on top with a knife
  9. Place on a lined tray and bake 18–20 min until edges are firm
  10. Cool fully on the tray — they crisp up as they cool
  11. Store in an airtight jar — keeps 7–10 days
